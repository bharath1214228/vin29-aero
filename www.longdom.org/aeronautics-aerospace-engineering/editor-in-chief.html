Biography

Dr. Omid Gohardani holds a doctorate in Aerospace Engineering from Cranfield University, United Kingdom and dual Master of Science degrees in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Arizona, United States and Master of Science degree in Aeronautical Engineering from Royal Institute of Technology, Sweden, following a visiting scholar position at the University of Florida, Florida, United States.He is a United States Wakonse teaching fellow, holds more than 15 national/international scholarships/awards and speaks six different languages fluently. He currently serves as a reviewer and editorial board member of several scientific journals and as a judge for the American Institute of Aeronautics and Astronautics student contests. In 2008, Dr. Gohardani was bestowed the title Honorary Citizen of Tucson, Arizona, United States and upon completion of a distinctive teaching program in Learner-Centered Education, awarded a College Teaching Certificate from the University of Arizona (summa cum laude).

Research Interest

Fluid mechanics, aerodynamics, fluid-structure interaction, droplet dynamics, advanced materials, aircraft icing, erosion, carbon nanotubes
